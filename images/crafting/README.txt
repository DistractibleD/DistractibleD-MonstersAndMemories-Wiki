Recipe screenshots go in this folder, named to match the "image" path
listed for that recipe in crafting.json (at the root of the project).

Recipe screenshots are saved as .jpg at quality 90, not .png — see
CLAUDE.md ("Item screenshot format") for why; the same reasoning
applies here.

To add a new recipe to the Crafting page, add an entry to crafting.json
(with a "tradeskill" matching one of the names in tradeskills.json) and
drop its screenshot here with a matching filename. No other files need
to change.
