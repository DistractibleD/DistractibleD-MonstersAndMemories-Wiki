Predicted missing items
========================

Built by looking for name patterns already in items.json (material/adjective
+ item type) and finding gaps in the set. This is inference from names/slots
we already have on file, not a guess at stats — no screenshots were opened
for this (per the inbox-only rule), so treat these as "things to watch for
in the inbox," not confirmed items.

HIGH CONFIDENCE — weapon material tiers
----------------------------------------
Corroded Bronze has a full 11-weapon set: Axe, Battle Axe, Dagger, Great
Scythe, Greatsword, Longsword, Maul, Scimitar, Scythe, Shortsword, Trident.

Copper only has 1 of those 11 (Copper Maul). Steel only has 1 (Steel
Longsword). Since one material tier has the complete set, the other two
tiers were very likely sold/dropped with the same 11 weapon types. Probable
missing items:

  Copper:  Axe, Battle Axe, Dagger, Great Scythe, Greatsword, Longsword,
           Scimitar, Scythe, Shortsword, Trident
  Steel:   Axe, Battle Axe, Dagger, Great Scythe, Greatsword, Maul,
           Scimitar, Scythe, Shortsword, Trident

MEDIUM CONFIDENCE — armor slot gaps within a material line
-------------------------------------------------------------
Rawhide (all Armor) currently covers: Waist, Feet, Wrist, Hands, Neck,
Face, Shirt — 7 of the common armor slots. Head and Chest are the two
common slots with no Rawhide piece yet, which stands out since almost
every other slot is filled. Watch for a Rawhide Head piece and a Rawhide
Chest piece.

Tattered Wool (all Armor) only covers Head, Hands, Shirt — much sparser
than Rawhide's 7 slots despite looking like a parallel starter-tier line.
Feet/Waist/Wrist/Neck/Face pieces may exist and just haven't come in yet.

Corroded Bronze also has two 2-piece armor sub-lines that may not be
complete: "Plate" (Boots/Feet, Collar/Neck) and "Chain" (Gloves/Hands,
Waistguard/Waist). Copper has one Plate piece (Breastplate/Chest) under a
different naming style ("Enchanted Copper Plate Breastplate"), so the
Copper/Steel Plate and Chain sub-lines may also be broader than what we
have — lower confidence than the weapon list since we only have 1-2 data
points per tier here, not a full template to compare against.

LOW CONFIDENCE — noted, not enumerated
-----------------------------------------
- Jewelry follows an "Inlaid [Gem] [Metal] [Ring|Earring]" pattern (gems
  seen: Black Pearl, Cat's Eye Agate, Malachite; metals seen: Copper, Iron,
  Silver). Combinations not yet seen probably exist, but the combo space is
  large enough that listing them out would be guessing rather than
  inferring — just flag new Ring/Earring screenshots as likely part of this
  family rather than assuming a specific missing combo.
- "Fine Wood" material items (Worn Fine Wood Bow, Worn Fine Wood Buckler,
  Cracked Fine Wood Staff) — only 2-3 items, not enough to see a template
  yet. Worth re-checking this list once a few more show up.

This file should be revisited whenever a batch of inbox items is processed —
cross the item off here if it turns out to match, or add newly-discovered
material/type patterns to the templates above.
