Items with missing information
=========================================

Tracks items.json entries that are missing data (not screenshot cropping — the site
renders cards from items.json, not the screenshot, so only missing data matters).

2026-07-15, second vendor sweep: a large batch of Used Weapons Dealer/Plate Armorer/
Weaponsmith/gem-vendor screenshots (name + price only, no stat cards) added ~185 more
`needsInfo: true` placeholder entries, following the exact same pattern as the batch below —
minimal safely-inferable fields only (type/slot/skill/twoHanded for weapons by matching an
established same-name-pattern sibling; type/slot only for armor by the piece-type-implies-
slot convention, e.g. every "Chain Coif" across every material tier is Head; nothing at all
beyond type for raw materials). Notable new discoveries this sweep, none with any stat data
yet: a full "Chain" armor line (Boots/Cloak/Coif/Gambeson/Gloves/Gorget/Leggings/Mask/
Shoulderguards/Tunic/Waistguard/Wristguard) across Copper/Bronze/Corroded Bronze/Iron/Rusty
Iron/Rusty Steel/Tarnished Bronze/Tarnished Iron tiers; a parallel full "Plate" armor line
(Arming Doublet/Boots/Bracer/Breastplate/Cloak/Collar/Gauntlets/Girdle/Greaves/Helm/
Pauldrons/Visor) across the same tiers; three raw-gem quality tiers (Chipped/Flawed/
Imperfect) across ~15 gem types each; and scrap/ore/bar smelting materials (Iron/Copper/
Gold/Silver/Tin Scraps, Gold/Silver/Tin/Pyrite Ore, Gold/Silver/Tin Bar). "Gambeson"/"Arming
Doublet" were both mapped to the Shirt slot (the padded under-armor layer, distinct from the
Chest-slot Tunic/Breastplate worn over it) — an inference from the pattern, not a confirmed
card, flag if it turns out wrong. A few gaps caught late from the *first* sweep were folded
in here too: Copper War Lance, Iron Battle Axe, and Iron Scimitar were vendor-confirmed
twice over but had been missed when the first batch was written up.
