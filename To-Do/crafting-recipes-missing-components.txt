Crafted items missing all or part of their components
=========================================

Tracks crafting.json recipes with no `components` list at all (or, for the two flagged
below, a full card but the components still missing). Most of these are minimal entries
seeded from a crafting-window screenshot (name/tradeskill/difficulty/listOrder only) —
per CLAUDE.md, a real recipe card fills in weight/size/components/image later. This file
exists so a future recipe-card screenshot can be matched back to what's still missing.
