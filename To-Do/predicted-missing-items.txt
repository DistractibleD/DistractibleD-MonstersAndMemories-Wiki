Predicted missing items
========================

Built by looking for name patterns already in items.json (material/adjective
+ item type) and finding gaps in the set. This is inference from names/slots
we already have on file, not a guess at stats — no screenshots were opened
for this beyond what came through the inbox, so treat these as "things to
watch for in the inbox," not confirmed items.

Last updated: 2026-07-14, after cross-referencing items.json against a "Used
Weapons Dealer" vendor's full sell list (name + price only, no stats — see
To-Do/items-needing-text.txt for the confirmed-but-dataless items this
revealed). items.json had grown substantially since the 2026-07-02 version of
this section below, so most of that version's "probable missing" Copper/Steel
guesses turned out to already be filled in independently — this rewrite
reflects current reality rather than patching the stale numbers in place.

Scythe, Greatsword, Kite Shield, Long Spear, Longsword, Mace, Maul, Scimitar,
Scythe, Shortsword, Spear, Tower Shield, Trident, War Lance, Warhammer.

complete sets, so any tier missing pieces from this list is a good bet to
still get them eventually. Current per-tier status (2026-07-14):

  Iron (plain):   only Axe has an items.json entry; the vendor batch

MEDIUM CONFIDENCE — armor slot gaps within a material line
-------------------------------------------------------------
Three separate "starter tier" armor lines exist (Rawhide, Tattered Wool,
Tattered Hide) — confirmed distinct because Tattered Hide Mask/Gorget have
different AC than Rawhide Mask/Gorget despite matching slot/weight/class,
so these are not naming inconsistencies for the same items.

  Rawhide (7 slots):        Waist, Feet, Wrist, Hands, Neck, Face, Shirt
  Tattered Wool (6 slots):  Head, Shoulders, Chest, Wrist, Hands, Shirt
  Tattered Hide (4 slots):  Head, Face, Neck, Shirt

Cross-referencing the three, the slots most likely to have a missing piece
in each line (i.e. covered by at least one other line but not this one):
  - Rawhide is missing:       Head, Chest, Shoulders
  - Tattered Wool is missing: Waist, Feet, Neck, Face
  - Tattered Hide is missing: Waist, Feet, Wrist, Hands, Shoulders, Chest
    (only 4 confirmed pieces so far, so this is the least certain of the
    three — could just mean fewer of this tier have been screenshotted yet)

Corroded Bronze also has two 2-piece armor sub-lines that may not be
complete: "Plate" (Boots/Feet, Collar/Neck) and "Chain" (Gloves/Hands,
Waistguard/Waist). Copper has one Plate piece (Breastplate/Chest) under a
different naming style ("Enchanted Copper Plate Breastplate"), so the
Copper/Steel Plate and Chain sub-lines may also be broader than what we
have — lower confidence than the weapon list since we only have 1-2 data
points per tier here, not a full template to compare against.

LOW CONFIDENCE — noted, not enumerated
-----------------------------------------
- Jewelry follows an "Inlaid [Gem] [Metal] [Ring|Earring]" pattern (gems
  seen: Black Pearl, Cat's Eye Agate, Malachite; metals seen: Copper, Iron,
  Silver). Combinations not yet seen probably exist, but the combo space is
  large enough that listing them out would be guessing rather than
  inferring — just flag new Ring/Earring screenshots as likely part of this
  family rather than assuming a specific missing combo.
- RESOLVED 2026-07-14 (moved out of low confidence): the "Fine Wood
  Worn/Cracked" question above is answered — it's not a per-item coin flip.
  A vendor's sell list confirmed the same Bow/Buckler/Great Staff = "Worn",
  (non-great) Staff = "Cracked" split holds across 4 parallel materials now
  (plain, Fine Wood, Elderwood, Ironbark): Worn Bow/Buckler/Great Staff,
  Worn Fine Wood Bow/Buckler/Great Staff, Worn Elderwood Bow/Buckler/Great
  Staff, Worn Ironbark Bow/Buckler/Great Staff, and Cracked
  Staff/Fine Wood Staff/Elderwood Staff/Ironbark Staff — 16 items forming a
  clean 4-material x 4-type grid with the condition word fixed per type, not
  per material. Several of these still have no items.json entry yet (see
  To-Do/items-needing-text.txt) but the naming pattern itself is now
  confirmed, not a guess — watch for the same "Worn Bow/Buckler/Great Staff
  + Cracked Staff" quartet on any new material that shows up.
