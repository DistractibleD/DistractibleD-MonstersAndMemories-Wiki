# Stone Golem

*This is an example page — replace it with your own content, or delete it once you have real pages.*

A slow but heavily armored boss found in the Quarry region.

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Health | 1,800 |
| Weakness | Fire |
| Resistance | Physical |

## Strategy

Keep your distance during the ground-slam attack (telegraphed by a red glow on its fists), then close in for 2–3 hits before it recovers.
