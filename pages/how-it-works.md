# How This Wiki Works

This wiki is a simple website — there's no login system, and pages aren't edited live on the site itself.

Instead, the page content lives in plain text files behind the scenes. New information and screenshots get added by the wiki's maintainer, then the site updates automatically.

If you've got information, a screenshot, or a correction to contribute, reach out to the maintainer directly (or through wherever you found this wiki) and it'll get added.
